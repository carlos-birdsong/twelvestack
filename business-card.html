Canva Setup Tips

In Canva, start with Business Card preset (3.5 × 2 in). Set background to #0a0c10. All three fonts (DM Serif Display, Outfit, JetBrains Mono) are available in Canva Pro. For the vertical divider, use a line element set to #c4956a at about 25% opacity. For the horizontal gold accent line under the name, use a rectangle shape set to #c4956a, roughly 28px wide by 1.5px tall. For the subtle glow under the wordmark, add a circle with #c4956a at 8-12% opacity, blur it, and center it below the logo text. Export as PDF Print with bleed marks for production.
